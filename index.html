/ faq

Questions we hear.

If you don't see yours, ask in the quote form or by phone — we read everything.
What does a quote actually include?+

A walkthrough, a written scope of work, and a fixed monthly price for recurring contracts (or per-project for one-time work). No hidden fees, no auto-renewal traps.

Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?+

Yes — vacuums, microfiber, chemicals. Green-certified products by default. We can match any procurement standards your facility requires.

Are you actually licensed, bonded, and insured?+

Yes. General liability and workers' compensation. COI sent directly to your office or property manager on request.

How fast can you start?+

Most accounts within a week of a signed proposal. Larger facilities or post-construction starts may need 10–14 days to staff properly.

What if we have a problem?+

Tell us. Owner-direct phone is on every invoice. Inside 24 hours, we re-clean anything that didn't meet spec — at no charge.

Do you serve buildings outside the listed cities?+

Almost certainly yes. We're Hayden-based with direct routes throughout North Idaho, plus vetted subcontractor partners covering everything south to Boise. If your facility is in Idaho, ask — we'll be straight about whether it's direct or partner-served.
