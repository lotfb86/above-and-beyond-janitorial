- **Primary:** "Water Damage Restoration Service" (higher value leads)

- Rotate: Updates, Offers, Events, Services
- Every post needs a CTA button
- Include local keyword naturally
- High-res images at 1200x900px (4:3)
- Front-load important info in first 150 characters

### Photos (upload weekly)
- Branded vehicles, equipment, team photos
- Before/after restoration work
- Action shots on job sites
- Minimum 10+ to start, add new weekly
- Video clips (Google favors video)

### Q&A Section (pre-seed 10-15 questions)
- "Do you offer 24/7 emergency water damage service?"
- "What areas do you serve?"
- "Are you licensed and insured?"
- "How quickly can you respond?"
- "Do you work with insurance companies?"

## REVIEW ACQUISITION SYSTEM

### Multi-Touch Approach
1. **In-Person** (immediately after service): Verbal ask + promise to send link
2. **SMS** (24-48 hours later): Short text with direct review link
3. **Email** (5-7 days later): "How did we do?" with review link

### Direct Review Link
Get from GBP "Ask for reviews" section: `https://g.page/r/YOUR-ID/review`

### Compliance
- NEVER offer incentives for reviews
- NEVER gate reviews (ask everyone, not just happy customers)
- NEVER create fake reviews
- ALWAYS respond to every review within 24 hours

### Targets
- 5-10 new reviews per month minimum
- Maintain 4.5+ star rating
- Ask for reviews immediately after emergency restoration (highest emotional gratitude)

## MULTI-CITY SEO (Avoiding Cannibalization)

### URL Structure
```
/services/water-damage-restoration/           (main hub - "water damage restoration North Idaho")
/services/water-damage-restoration/coeur-dalene/   (targets "water damage restoration Coeur d'Alene")
/services/water-damage-restoration/spokane/         (targets "water damage restoration Spokane")
```

### Each City Page MUST Have Unique
- H1, title tag, meta description
- Opening paragraph
- Case studies/examples from that city
- Local references (landmarks, neighborhoods)
- Local testimonials
- At least 50% unique FAQs
- 800-1,500 words of genuine content

### Internal Linking
- City pages link UP to parent service hub
- Hub page links DOWN to each city page
- City pages cross-link laterally ("Also serving nearby...")
- Blog posts link to relevant city pages
