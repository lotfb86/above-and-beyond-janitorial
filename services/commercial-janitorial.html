Questions we hear.

How often do you clean?+

Most accounts run 5+ nights/week (after-hours). We also offer 2–4 nights/week, weekly, or monthly cadence — whatever fits the building's traffic.

Who supplies the chemicals and equipment?+

We do. Green-certified by default. We can match your facility's procurement standards (low-VOC, EPA Safer Choice, etc.) on request.

Is it the same crew every night?+

Yes. The same team for the life of the contract — they learn your building. Crew tenure averages 3.5 years.

How are missed items handled?+

Call. Inside 24 hours we re-clean it at no charge. No invoice tricks, no minimum clauses.
